Fluffy Pancakes

IngredientQuantity
Flour1 cup
Milk3/4 cup
Egg1
Baking Powder2 tsp

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat and grease it lightly.
  3. Pour batter into the pan and cook until bubbles form.
  4. Flip and cook for another minute.
